Local Dope Wars is an open-source React and TypeScript remake of the classic trading game, rebuilt as a fast browser experience instead of a thin nostalgia wrapper. It keeps the core tension of buying low, traveling, dodging risk, and cashing out before the clock runs out, then layers in cleaner presentation, a dedicated SVG map, browser-local autosave, and run scoring.

The current release already reaches beyond a single starter map. Players can choose between bundled Gwinnett County and Atlanta Intown packs, import custom local content packs from JSON, and play through market shocks, collector pressure, cop stops, rival-crew ambushes, debt, banking, pawn advances, gear management, and local high-score chasing.

That combination of classic arcade economics and expandable location-based content is what makes the project worth watching. Local Dope Wars is not just a remake of an old loop. It is a framework for turning regional flavor, alternate maps, and player-made scenarios into new runs without rewriting the whole game from scratch.
